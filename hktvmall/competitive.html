Marketing & E-Commerce Strategy

Digital marketing channels, content integration, and customer engagement tactics

Marketing Strategy Components

HKTVLive Streaming Commerce

100 Concurrent Channels: Live shopping platform where merchants directly interact with viewers, demonstrate products, and offer exclusive deals. Integrated with HKTVmall for instant purchases. Drives engagement and impulse buying through real-time interaction.

HKTV LOOK Video Platform

Video-on-Demand Content: Launched in 2024, this platform enables customers to watch content creator videos and HKTVLive replays anytime. Features "Play to Buy" - one-click purchase of products mentioned in videos. Combines entertainment with shopping.

Multimedia Advertising

HK$156.5M Revenue (2024): Merchants advertise through banners, sponsored listings, HKTVLive promotions, and video content. Platform leverages 1.6M monthly active users for targeted digital advertising. Multiple ad formats and interactive tools available.

Social Commerce Features

ShareHub & ChicChat: ShareHub virtual community for merchants and customers to share content. ChicChat enables in-app group messaging between merchants and customers for direct communication and promotions. EcoMart second-hand marketplace promotes sustainability.

MTR & Outdoor Campaigns

Mega Advertising Reach: "Mom-certified" campaign (2024) covered 58 MTR stations with 3,120 billboards, won 4 awards at Kam Fan Awards 2023. Distributed HK$3,000 discount vouchers across Hong Kong. Dominates public transportation advertising.

Omnichannel O2O Strategy

10+ O2O Concept Stores: Physical stores in North Point, Shatin, South Horizons, etc. Customers can touch and feel products, then order online for home delivery or store pickup. Seamless integration between online and offline experiences.
