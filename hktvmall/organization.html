Organization Profile

Comprehensive overview of HKTVmall's background, business model, and market position

Company Background & History

1992 - City Telecom Founded

The company was initially incorporated as City Telecom (H.K.) Limited, focusing on telecommunications services.

2000-2011 - Telecom & Broadband Era

Launched Hong Kong Broadband Network (HKBN), becoming a pioneer in fiber-optic broadband services. Achieved milestones including 1Gbps broadband for HK$199/month and surpassed 1 million subscribers in 2009.

2012 - Strategic Transformation

Sold HKBN and all telecom businesses to CVC Capital Partners, a global private equity firm. Pivoted to multimedia production and distribution.

2013 - Renamed to HKTV

City Telecom renamed as Hong Kong Television Network Limited (HKTV), focusing on multimedia content creation and OTT platform development.

2014 - Multimedia Launch

Grand launch of OTT content platform. Started trial run for online shopping mall in December 2014.

February 2015 - HKTVmall E-commerce Launch

Grand launch of online shopping mall "HKTVmall" with the slogan "We Sell Whatever You Can Imagine", partnering with 333+ stores from Hong Kong, Japan, and Korea.

2016-2019 - Rapid Expansion

Expanded warehouse and logistics center to Tsing Yi (144,000 sq ft). Implemented automated robotic picking & warehousing system. Opened 10 O2O Concept Stores across Hong Kong.

2020 - Pandemic Acceleration

Achieved record-breaking sales performance. Ranked first in Top Trending Shopping Sites (Google Hong Kong Year In Search 2020). Expanded delivery network to South Lantau and remote areas.

2021 - Technology Venture Transformation

Renamed to Hong Kong Technology Venture Company Limited. Launched EcoMart second-hand marketplace and HKTV Ecommerce Academy. Filed first Hong Kong patent for Fully Automated Retail Store.

2022 - Innovation Milestones

Launched "Wet Market Express" (3-hour fresh food delivery), ShareHub virtual community, HKTVmall Supermarket in Tseung Kwan O, and "UK Store by HKTVmall".

2023 - Global & Tech Expansion

Launched Everuts (cross-border e-commerce), HKTVLive (100 concurrent live channels), 3PL service center in Tsing Yi, ThePlace eStore solution, and first UK automated retail store ("in:Five" in Manchester).

2024 - 10th Anniversary

Celebrated 10 years of HKTVmall. Launched ChicChat in-app messaging, HKTV LOOK video platform, HKTVplus membership, CASHBACK store model. Achieved HK$8.59B GMV. Proposed special dividend of HK$0.38 per share.

Business Model & Operations

Hybrid E-Commerce Model

HKTVmall operates a sophisticated dual-model approach that combines the strengths of both direct retail and marketplace platforms:

1P Business (Direct Merchandise Sales)

28.7% of GMV
  • Direct inventory control
  • Pricing flexibility
  • Quality assurance
  • Focus on essential items & groceries
  • Gross profit margin: 25.4% (2024)

HKTVmall directly purchases, stocks, and sells products - ensuring consistent supply of high-demand items, particularly groceries that drive traffic.

3P Business (Merchant Concessionaire Sales)

71.3% of GMV
  • Thousands of merchant partners
  • 2.2 million product SKUs
  • Long tail effect leverage
  • Commission-based revenue
  • Blended commission rate: 22.3% (2024)

Third-party merchants sell through HKTVmall platform, paying commissions. This expands product variety without inventory risk, offering consumers unparalleled choice.

Strategic Benefits of Hybrid Model

Risk Balance: 1P provides stability, 3P offers variety without inventory burden

Margin Optimization: Combined gross margin improved to 23.2% in 2024

Customer Satisfaction: One-stop shop with essential items always in stock

Scalability: Easy expansion through merchant recruitment
