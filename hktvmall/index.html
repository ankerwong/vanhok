Executive Summary

Company Overview

Hong Kong Technology Venture Company Limited (HKTVmall) has transformed from a telecommunications company into Hong Kong's largest online shopping platform since its e-commerce launch in February 2015. Celebrating its 10th anniversary in 2024, HKTVmall has established itself as a market leader with a comprehensive digital ecosystem.

Business Performance

In 2024, HKTVmall achieved a Gross Merchandise Value (GMV) of HK$8,589.8 million, representing a 2.0% year-on-year growth despite challenging economic conditions. The platform serves 1,519,000 unique customers with 1.6 million monthly active app users, demonstrating strong market penetration.

Technology Innovation

HKTVmall operates one of Asia's most advanced e-commerce infrastructures, featuring fully automated warehouses with robotic AGV systems, crossbelt sorters processing 13,000+ items per hour, and smart logistics delivering 50,000+ grocery items in as fast as 8 hours. The platform integrates cutting-edge technologies including AI-driven analytics, live streaming commerce (HKTVLive), and seamless omnichannel experiences.

Competitive Positioning

With a 9.2% traffic share in Hong Kong's e-commerce market, HKTVmall ranks as a top-4 player behind Taobao (20.9%), Amazon (11%), and Price (9.8%). The platform's competitive advantages include local market expertise, integrated content-commerce ecosystem, advanced automation technology, and strong brand recognition (ranked #2 in YouGov Recommend Rankings 2022).

Business Model

HKTVmall operates a hybrid model combining Direct Merchandise Sales (1P - 28.7%) and Merchant Concessionaire Sales (3P - 71.3%). This dual approach ensures consistent supply of essential items while offering extensive product variety through 2.2 million SKUs across categories including Groceries (42.5% of GMV), Beauty & Health (25.2%), and Digital & Electronics (10.2%).

Strategic Initiatives

Beyond core e-commerce, HKTVmall has launched innovative ventures: Wet Market Express (3-hour fresh food delivery covering 90%+ customers), Everuts (global personal shopping with HK$59.5M GMV and 95.7% YoY growth), and investments in life science research. The company's vision of "Always Something New" drives continuous innovation for long-term sustainability.
