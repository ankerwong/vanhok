Business Processes & Workflows

End-to-end process mapping and operational workflows at HKTVmall

Core Business Processes

Customer Journey Process

8-Step Flow: 1) Browse products (web/app) → 2) Search & filter → 3) Add to cart → 4) Checkout with payment → 5) Order processing → 6) Warehouse picking (AGV robots) → 7) Sorting & packing → 8) Delivery to customer. Average time: 3-8 hours for groceries.

Order Fulfillment Workflow

Automated Processing: Real-time inventory check → Automated warehouse assignment → Robotic AGV picking → Quality control → Crossbelt sorting (13,000+ items/hour) → Route optimization → Delivery dispatch. Fulfillment costs: 12.3% of GMV (2024).

Merchant Onboarding Process

3-Step Integration: 1) Application & approval → 2) Platform training (HKTV Ecommerce Academy) → 3) Store setup with product upload. Options: HKTVmall (3P), ThePlace (independent eStore), or 3PL service. Merchants choose fulfillment method: self-fulfill or HKTVmall warehousing.

Inventory Management Process

Real-Time System: Automated stock level monitoring → Demand forecasting with AI → Reorder point triggers → Supplier coordination → Inbound receiving → Warehouse storage optimization → Inventory turnover tracking. Supports 2.2M SKUs across 1P and 3P models.

Marketing Campaign Lifecycle

Campaign Management: Strategy planning → Creative development → Channel selection (HKTVLive, MTR ads, social media) → Launch & monitoring → Performance analytics → ROI assessment. Example: "Mom-certified" campaign reached 58 MTR stations, 3,120 billboards.

Customer Service Workflow

Multi-Channel Support: In-app chat → ChicChat group messaging → Email support → Phone hotline → FAQ knowledge base. Issue categories: order tracking, returns, product inquiries, technical support. Average response time optimized through automation and CRM integration.
