7. RISK ASSESSMENT

7.1 Identified Risks and Mitigation Strategies

Risk Category Probability Impact Mitigation Strategy
Weather Disruption Medium High Comprehensive weather protection, contingency venue arrangements, weather insurance coverage
Low Attendance Low High Intensive marketing campaign, early bird pricing, group discounts, strong social media presence
Sponsorship Shortfall Medium Medium Over-recruitment of potential sponsors, flexible package options, backup revenue streams
Vendor/Supplier Issues Low Medium Multiple supplier contracts, early delivery schedules, quality assurance protocols
Regulatory Changes Low Medium Early permit applications, legal compliance review, government liaison coordination
Cost Overruns Medium Low 10% contingency budget, fixed-price contracts where possible, regular budget monitoring

7.2 Financial Risk Management

  • Contingency Reserve: HK$77,000 (10% of total budget) allocated for unexpected expenses
  • Payment Terms: Staggered vendor payments to maintain cash flow flexibility
  • Insurance Coverage: Comprehensive event insurance including public liability, weather protection, and cancellation coverage
  • Revenue Guarantees: Secure minimum sponsorship commitments before major expense commitments
  • Break-Even Buffer: Current projections show 35% margin above break-even point

8.3 Strategic Recommendations

8.3.1 Pre-Event Phase (3 months prior)

  • Prioritize Diamond and Gold sponsor acquisition for maximum revenue impact
  • Launch early bird ticket sales with 20% discount to accelerate cash flow
  • Establish vendor partnerships with fixed-price contracts to control costs
  • Implement comprehensive marketing strategy focusing on social media and educational partnerships

8.3.2 Revenue Optimization

  • Focus on high-margin merchandise sales through strategic placement and limited-edition items
  • Develop VIP experience packages to justify premium pricing
  • Create bundle deals combining tickets, food, and merchandise for increased average spend
  • Explore additional revenue streams such as photo packages or workshop fees

8.3.3 Cost Management

  • Negotiate volume discounts for merchandise production
  • Optimize staff scheduling to match attendance patterns
  • Implement efficient inventory management to minimize waste
  • Leverage volunteer programs to supplement paid staff where appropriate
